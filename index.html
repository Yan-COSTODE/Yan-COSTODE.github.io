About Me

My name is Yan Costode, and I am a dedicated game programmer with a strong passion for bringing digital worlds to life. My journey into game development started with a love for video games and a curiosity about how they are made. This curiosity led me to study game programming and development, where I honed my skills in languages like C++, C#, and various game engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine.

Beyond coding, I enjoy collaborating with artists, designers, and other developers to create cohesive and compelling gaming experiences. My goal is always to push the boundaries of what's possible and deliver games that players will love and remember.

When I'm not programming, you can find me exploring new games or tinkering with the latest technology. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I hope you enjoyed seeing the projects I've poured my heart into.
